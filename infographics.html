The Impacts

💼 Employment

Poor connectivity limits access to remote jobs and online markets, reducing economic opportunities in rural areas.

🎓 Education

Many rural students struggle with online classes due to weak internet, creating gaps in learning compared to urban peers.

🏥 Healthcare

Telemedicine and online consultations are harder to access, leaving rural communities with fewer health services.

💬 Inclusion

The digital divide deepens inequality, giving connected Filipinos more advantages in education, work, and services than those offline.

Solutions in Progress
🏗️

Infrastructure

Expanding internet access through broadband, satellites, and free Wi-Fi.

📜

Policy & Regulation

Laws and rules that ensure affordable and fair internet for all.

📚

Capacity Building

Training and programs that help people use digital tools effectively.
